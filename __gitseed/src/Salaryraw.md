## Key Observations

### **Standard Progression (GL01-GL14):**
- **15 steps** per grade level
- Full career progression over 15 years within each grade
- Consistent step increments ranging from ~1.2% to 1.7% per step
- Total of **210 salary points** across these grades

### **Senior Management (GL15-GL17):**
- **9 steps** per grade level (shorter progression)
- Reflects senior/director positions with limited longevity in same grade
- Higher starting salaries but fewer incremental steps
- Total of **27 salary points** across these three grades

### **Salary Increments:**
- **Intra-grade (steps):** Annual increments within same grade level
- **Inter-grade (promotions):** Larger jumps between grade levels
- **Pattern:** Increments increase at higher grade levels

### **Conversion to Monthly:**
To convert these annual salaries to monthly (for payroll):
```
Monthly Salary = Annual Salary ÷ 12
```

**Examples:**
- GL01 Step 01: ₦907,490 ÷ 12 = ₦75,624/month
- GL07 Step 01: ₦1,374,960 ÷ 12 = ₦114,580/month
