## Important Notes

1. **Structure Type:** CONPSS (Consolidated Public Service Salary Structure)
2. **Currency:** Nigerian Naira (₦)
3. **Pay Frequency:** Monthly (converted from annual)
4. **Original Data:** Annual salaries from official CONPSS document
5. **Conversion Method:** Annual ÷ 12, rounded to nearest whole number
6. **Grade Levels 1-14:** 15 steps each (standard progression)
7. **Grade Levels 15-17:** 9 steps each (senior management levels)
8. **Step Progression:** Annual increments move staff through steps
9. **Grade Progression:** Promotions move staff to next grade level

## Structure Breakdown

### **Standard Grades (GL01-GL14):**
- **15 steps** per grade level
- **210 total salary points** (14 grades × 15 steps)
- Entry to mid-senior management levels
- Full career progression pathway

### **Senior Grades (GL15-GL17):**
- **9 steps** per grade level
- **27 total salary points** (3 grades × 9 steps)
- Senior management to director levels
- Shorter progression due to seniority

### **Total Structure:**
- **17 grade levels** (GL01-GL17)
- **237 total salary points** (210 + 27)
- Comprehensive coverage for all public service positions
